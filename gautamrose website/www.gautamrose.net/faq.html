Q.6 Is it safe to use my credit/ debit card on gautamrose.net?

Ans.6

Yes it is absolutely safe to use your card on gautamrose.net. A recent directive from RBI makes it mandatory to have an additional authentication pass code verified by VISA (VBV) or MSC (Master Secure Code) which has to be entered by online shoppers while paying online using visa or master credit card. It means extra security for customers, thus making online shopping safer.

Q.7 Are there any other charges or taxes in addition to the price shown?

Ans.7

We made free shipping on orders of Rs.1001/or more. For COD method customer will be charged additional Rs.30/as COD charges which will be mentioned in the invoice. Free COD option is available on order of Rs.1000/or more.

Ans.8

Cash on delivery means that you can pay for your order at the time of order delivery at your doorstep. COD can be used as payment method only for an order not exceeding Rs. 10,000.

Ans.11

Email contains your order ID number and the shipping method, AWB no. with tracking link. Keep tracking it by clicking on 'Action Button' in 'My Orders'. Q.13 What if my package is opened/tampered with?

Ans.13

If you feel that your package was opened/tampered, please don't accept the delivery. Please raise the Refund request on the 'Ticket Panel' in 'My Account' on your dashboard. We will confirm your Refund request (ticket) via email/phone call and may process the refund of order amount or resend order as per your wish/convenience.

Q.14 When can I expect refund for my returned item?

Ans.14

For prepaid orders, it will be initiated immediately to card/bank account. Your bank will take a maximum of 4 business days to credit this amount. We will also keep you updated on email and SMS.

Q.15 When can I expect refund for my cancelled order?

Ans.15

If you cancel the order before shipping, we would start the refund almost immediately. In case you cancel the order after shipment, it might take up to 6 days to initiate the refund.

Q.16 How can I cancel my order?

Ans.16 You may cancel your order by clicking on cancel button available under customers order details in "My Account " menu with providing an appropriate reason for cancelling the order.

Q.17 How can I return my order?

Ans.17 
You may return your order at the time of delivery and generate the ticket for refund or it will automatically refunded  once we receive your returned order/consignment. 
Order can not be returned or cancelled after accepting the delivery of order.
